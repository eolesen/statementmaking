Interested in Wearable Computing?

Consider participating in Statement Making, an event that showcases student-made wearable tech and digitally fabricated pieces of clothing.

Statement Making is a collaborative event run by CCI Makerspace and Rowe Arts Fab Lab. It is a way to generate excitement about digital fabrication and encourage people from different departments to meet each other. Anyone can work in a group or alone to create any sort of wearable device or piece of clothing, it just has to incorporate some interactive element or digital fabrication technology. It could be anything from 3D printing, laser cutting, LED lights, interaction with the environment, or data from the web. Come to our info session to see what is possible. No experience necessary, we provide training too!
